Capudino

Capudino Studios was founded in 2018.
We are an independent game developer and publisher studio, based in Brasília, Brazil, formed by five friends and college colleagues that share a love for pixel art and 3D toon art.
Our goal is to create games that can be played and enjoyed by all, without giving up of creating a healthy studio and a respectful community.
